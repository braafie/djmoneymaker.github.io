BIO

MoneyMaker (aka Ashwad), also known for his work in the remix project with well-known producer DJ Chello. He is a Producer and DJ based out of Bergsig, Springbok but moved to Cape Town round about Jan 2017. He creates and performs verious music, including unofficial remixes of club and hip hop favorites, original soulful bootlegs, funky house or amalgamations of other styles. Starting his career as a DJ at a well known Local radio station, covering styles and genres with a focus on club music and hip hop.
